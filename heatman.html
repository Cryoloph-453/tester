HEAT MAN

BOSS SELECT

Metal Man Quick Man Bubble Man Wood Man Air Man Heat Man Crash Man Flash Man

OVERVIEW

Heat Man is a fire based robot master in Mega Man 2. He is the sixth boss in the game. Heat Man is equipped with a powerful heat-based weapon called the Heat Chip, which allows him to shoot heaten chips that can trap and damage Mega Man. He is also known for his strength and ability to withstand powerful attacks, making him a challenging boss to defeat.

WEAKNESS

Heat Man is weak against the Metal Blade, which is the weapon obtained from defeating Metal Man. The Metal Blade is effective against Heat Man because it can break through his heat-based attacks and deal significant damage. Using the Metal Blade against Heat Man can make the battle easier and increase the chances of victory.

STRENGTH

Heat Man is strong against Quick Man, Air Man, and Heat Man. This is because his heat Chip weapon can trap and damage these bosses, making it difficult for them to attack effectively.

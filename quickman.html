QUICK MAN

BOSS SELECT

Metal Man Quick Man Bubble Man Wood Man Air Man Heat Man Crash Man Flash Man

OVERVIEW

Quick Man's main weapon is the Quick Boomerang, a fast-moving projectile that can be thrown at enemies. He is knonw for his speed and agility as well as being a very arrogant character in the manga, often being overconfident. He is the fastest of all the Robot Masters and he is often very impulsive.

WEAKNESS

Quick Man is vulnerable to attacks from his own weapon and certain other bosses. This includes attacks from Air Man, Wood Man, and Flash Man weapons.

STRENGTH

Quick Man is known for his speed and agility, making him a fast and elusive opponent. His weapon is strong against Metal Man, Bubble Man, and Heat Man.

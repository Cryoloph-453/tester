FLASH MAN

BOSS SELECT

Metal Man Quick Man Bubble Man Wood Man Air Man Heat Man Crash Man Flash Man

OVERVIEW

Flash Man is the ninth boss in the Mega Man series. He is a robot who can move at extremely high speeds and fire rapid shots. His stage is a dark, mysterious place with many traps and hazards.

WEAKNESS

Flash Man is vulnerable to attacks from below, as his armor is less protected in that area. He is also vulnerable to being hit by his own rapid shots if he isn't careful. He is weak against attacks from the Metal Blade and the Crash Bomb.

STRENGTH

Flash Man is strong in terms of speed and mobility, allowing him to quickly traverse the stage and evade attacks. His rapid-fire weapon is effective at dealing damage from a distance. His Time Stopper is effective against Quick Man.

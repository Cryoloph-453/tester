METAL MAN

BOSS SELECT

Metal Man Quick Man Bubble Man Wood Man Air Man Heat Man Crash Man Flash Man

OVERVIEW

Metal Man is the first boss in Mega Man 2. He is a robot master created by Dr. Wily and is designed to be a master of metal. He is equipped with a powerful weapon called the Metal Blade, which can cut through almost anything. He is also known for his high defense and can withstand a lot of damage. Metal Man is a formidable opponent and can be a challenge to defeat, especially for new players. However, once defeated, he can be a valuable ally in the fight against Dr. Wily and his other robot masters.

WEAKNESS

Metal Man is weak to his own weapon, the Metal Blade. And he is also weak to the Air Shooter and Mega Buster.

STRENGTH

His weapon is strong against a majority of the bosses. This makes him the first boss that most players will face.

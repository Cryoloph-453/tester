Air MAN

BOSS SELECT

Metal Man Quick Man Bubble Man Wood Man Air Man Heat Man Crash Man Flash Man

OVERVIEW

Air Man is a wind-based robot master in Mega Man 2. He is the fifth boss in the game. Air Man is equipped with a powerful wind-based weapon called the Air Shooter, which allows him to shoot air bullets that can push Mega Man around the screen. He is also known for his speed and agility, making him a challenging boss to defeat.

WEAKNESS

Air Man is weak against the Bubble Lead, which is the weapon obtained from defeating Bubble Man. The Bubble Lead is effective against Air Man because it can slow down his speed and agility, making it easier to hit him. Using the Bubble Lead against Air Man can make the battle easier and increase the chances of victory.

STRENGTH

Air Man is strong against Heat Man and Wood Man. This is because his air bullets can push these bosses around the screen, making it difficult for them to attack effectively.

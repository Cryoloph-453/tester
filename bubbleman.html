BUBBLE MAN

BOSS SELECT

Metal Man Quick Man Bubble Man Wood Man Air Man Heat Man Crash Man Flash Man

OVERVIEW

Bubble Man is the third boss in Mega Man 2. He is a robot master created by Dr. Wily and is designed to be a formidable opponent for Mega Man. Bubble Man is equipped with a powerful water-based weapon called the Bubble Lead, which allows him to shoot bubbles that can trap and damage Mega Man. He is also known for his agility and ability to move quickly through water, making him a challenging boss to defeat.

WEAKNESS

Bubble Man's weakness is the Metal Blade, which is the weapon obtained from defeating Metal Man. The Metal Blade is effective against Bubble Man because it can break through his bubble attacks and deal significant damage. Using the Metal Blade against Bubble Man can make the battle easier and increase the chances of victory.

STRENGTH

Bubble Man is strong against Quick Man, Air Man, and Heat Man. This is because his Bubble Lead weapon can trap and damage these bosses, making it difficult for them to attack effectively.

CRASH MAN

BOSS SELECT

Metal Man Quick Man Bubble Man Wood Man Air Man Heat Man Crash Man Flash Man

OVERVIEW

Crash Man is the eighth boss in the Mega Man series. He is a robot who can crash into his enemies and deal significant damage. His stage is a large, industrial facility with many obstacles and hazards.

WEAKNESS

Crash Man is vulnerable to attacks from above, as his armor is less protected in that area. He is also vulnerable to being pushed off ledges or into hazards as well as the air shooter weapon.

STRENGTH

Crash Man is strong in close combat and can deal significant damage with his crash attacks. He is also durable and can withstand a considerable amount of damage before being defeated. His weapon is the Crash Bomber. The Crash Bomber is effective against Quick Man and Flash Man.
